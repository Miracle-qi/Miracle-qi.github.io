<section class="homepage-intro" id="about">
  <h2>About</h2>
  <p>
    I am a PhD candidate in the Control Systems group at TU Eindhoven, focusing on
    <strong>trustworthy decision-making and motion control</strong>. My research integrates temporal
    logic planning with safety-critical control to manage uncertainty and resolve deadlock in
    multi-agent interactions. I work at the intersection of
    <strong>control theory, game theory, formal methods, and reinforcement learning</strong>, with
    applications in autonomous driving and aerospace systems.
  </p>
</section>
